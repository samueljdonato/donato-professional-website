---
title: "Fungal Strain Informatics Platform"
---

**Overview**

- Designed and built a comprehensive strain management system supporting R&D and commercial-scale production operations
- Platform tracked over 700 fungal strain lines, integrating genomic data, growth metrics, and production outcomes

**Key Features**

- Cold storage cryo strain banking system
- Automated tracking of strain lineage and genealogy
- Active strain development and maintenance protocols
- Contamination tracking

**Data Architecture**

- SQL database with optimized schema for biological relationships
- Automated data analysis and organization scripts
- Real-time data synchronization across laboratory sites
- Automated backup and version control

**Analytics Dashboard**

- Interactive dashboards providing strain performance metrics across different growth conditions
- Production yield predictions

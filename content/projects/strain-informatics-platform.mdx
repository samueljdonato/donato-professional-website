title: "Fungal Strain Informatics Platform"

**Overview**

During my time at Ecovative, I designed and built a comprehensive strain management system that became the backbone of the company's R&D and production operations. This platform tracked over 700 fungal strain lines, integrating genomic data, growth metrics, and production outcomes.
<br />
**Key Features** 

-Cold storage cryo strain banking system

-Automated tracking of strain lineage and genealogy

-Active strain development and mainteance protocols

-Contamination tracking

<br />
**Data Architecture**

-SQL database with optimized schema for biological relationships

-Automated data analysis and organization scripts

-Real-time data synchronization across laboratory sites

-Automated backup and version control
<br />

**Analytics Dashboard**

-Built interactive dashboards providing

    -Strain performance metrics across different growth conditions

    -Production yield predictions
